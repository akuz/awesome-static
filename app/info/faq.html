Here is a list of frequently asked questions (FAQ) that we get from the clients and our professional service providers. Please feel free to contact us if your question is not listed below.

Clients Questions

What kind of locations can professionals visit?

Our professionals can visit your home, work place, or perform the activity outdoors (for personal training and other applicable activities). Please check at the booking stage which locations the professional accepts.

Are professionals employed by Awesome Treat™?

No. The professionals listed at Awesome Treat™ are not employed by us. They are independent professionals.

What services does Awesome Treat™ provide?

We check the quality of the professionals listed with us by doing their background check, security check, and qualifications verification. We also provide the schedule management and booking services through our app.

Professionals Questions

I want to list my profile here!

Please open the contact us page and visit our website to register as a professional. You will need to go through the process of background and qualifications check before you can start working.

How fast will I get the money I earn?

The funds will be transferred to your bank account a week after the appointment, provided the client didn't claim a refund, in which case we will need to take a longer time to investigate and make a decision on the claim. Your other unaffected payments will still be transferred after a week.

Will I be employed by Awesome Treat™?

No. We merely provide you with access to clients through our scheduling and booking services available in our app. You still have to pass our background and qualifications check though.

Do I have to pay the taxes?

Yes, you have to pay taxes on the money your earn. By the end of the tax year, you will have to register as self-employed with HMRC and complete your tax return.

Can you make me a working visa?

Sorry, no. You must already have permission to work in the location where you provide the services.
