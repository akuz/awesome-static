At Awesome Treat™ we connect people looking for massage, beauty treatments, and personal training with freelance providers of these services.

Our app helps to coordinate the schedule so that the clients know when the therapists are available, and can pre-book the most convenient time.

Star rating and reviews help the clients decide which therapist is best at what they do, and select the treatment best in its class.

Our app's geolocation capability lets you find the therapists nearest to you, who work in your area and will be able to get to you faster.

Online payment eliminates the need for clients to carry the cash, while providing a guarantee of reward for the therapists.
