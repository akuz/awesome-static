You can start your own business or just do some work on the side by listing your professional profile at Awesome Treat™.

We will help you to create your own work schedule and manage client reservations in the time slots you allocate as working time.

All clients, just like the service providers, also have a star rating and a feedback page. You can review them before accepting a reservation.

You can set your primary work area by choosing your location on a map (or using a postcode) and specifying the maximum distance you are willing to travel to reach clients.

We will take payments from the clients for you, which means you can accept all the major credit cards. The accumulated funds will be deposited to your account within a week of each service appointment on a rolling basis.

Taking payment online also protects you from the clients not respecting their commitment, and provides additional check that you will be dealing with a creditworthy person, whos details we have on record.
